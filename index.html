Related Projects

Activity Area Estimation of Students of Aoyama Gakuin University around Shibuya Area, Tokyo

I estimated 95% activity areas of Aoyama Gakuin University students from their Wi-Fi connection history in a part of my thesis.

I utilized kernel density estimation and minimum convex polygon (MCP) methods for this estimation

The data was collected under the users' consent and provided anonimously.
